Our Story

More Than a Cut.
It's a Ritual.

Castro Barbershop was built on one idea: every man deserves to leave looking and feeling his best. We blend old-school craftsmanship with modern technique to deliver cuts that turn heads.

Whether you're coming in for your weekly fresh-up or your first time in the chair, you'll get the same level of care, precision, and respect every single time.
